Safety in Urban Cycling

Safer streets for Cyclists and Pedestrians

Current Challenges

  • Unsafe roads for cyclists and pedestrians
  • Poorly marked crosswalks
  • Dangerous intersections

Solutions for Safety

  • Dedicated bike lanes
  • Improved intersection safety
  • Bike parking and storage

Additional efforts include:

  • Organizing workshops on cycling safety and infrastructure
  • Engaging city planners for better cycling infrastructure
  • Partnering with local schools and businesses to promote cycling

Why Advocate for Safe Cycling?

  • Improves public health
  • Reduces traffic congestion
  • Creates sustainable urban spaces

Smart cities lead to safer cycling!
